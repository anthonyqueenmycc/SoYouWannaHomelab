## Installing the Microsoft Visual C++ Redistributeable
1. Go ahead and click "OK" on the pop-up.<br>
<br>
<img width="371" height="241" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/81dbee62-099a-44ee-acfb-c48ec41b9135" /><br>
<br>
2. You should now see a message saying the VirtualBox Installer ended prematurely. Click "Finish". <br>
<br>
<img width="505" height="402" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc31a7de-f2a7-413c-9323-e0fce461cfe2" /><br>
<br>
3. You will see another error that says Installation failed! Click "OK".<br>
<br>
<img width="382" height="158" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bda3cd1c-0622-425e-b503-924115ff08bd" /><br>
<br>
4. Click <a href="https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/cpp/windows/latest-supported-vc-redist?view=msvc-170#latest-supported-redistributable-version" target="_blank"> HERE </a> to go to the Microsoft page that contains the installer. You want to choose the version labeled X64.<br>
<br>
<img width="696" height="361" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fcb74e08-07ed-49c6-b17e-efd3cc6f1481" /><br>
<br>
5. Once the download has finished, click on the executable to launch it.<br>
<br>
<img width="435" height="119" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4ebc00f5-a36d-4623-bdcf-6d90e0160900" /><br>
<br>
6. Once it launches, click the box to agree to the terms and conditions, then click "Install".<br>
<br>
<img width="478" height="299" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/89dbe435-9697-4857-9cea-1358808bbed2" /><br>
<br>
7. If you are presented with another User Account Control (UAC) message, click "Yes".<br>
<br>
<img width="464" height="411" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7bc9612d-f5c0-40c3-b6b4-46fff99a5759" /><br>
<br>
8. Once the installation has completed, click "Close".<br>
<br>
<img width="481" height="301" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1765c5d8-3a1a-4c2f-b2bc-4bb13a9f9e30" /><br>
<br>
9. You now have the Microsoft Visual C++ Redistributeable installed! Go ahead and launch the VirtualBox installer again (if you can't find it in your web browser, try checking your downloads folder!), then click <a href=https://github.com/anthonyqueenmycc/SoYouWannaHomelab/tree/main/01-Installing_VirtualBox#resume_install> HERE </a> to return to the instructions for installing VirtualBox.
