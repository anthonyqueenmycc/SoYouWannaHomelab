## Installing VirtualBox
The first step of creating a Homelab is to set up your hypervisor. In my class, we have opted to use VirtualBox due to it's wide compatibility with host and guest operating systems. <br>

1. Download VirtualBox. <br>
The first thing we need to do is go to the VirtualBox webpage. Click <a href="https://www.virtualbox.org/" target="_blank"> HERE </a> to go to their page and then click on the big blue button for the downloads section. (Tip: If you hold down the Ctrl key when you click on the link, it will open in a new tab instead of replacing this page!)<br>
<br>
<img width="1920" height="919" alt="www.virtualbox.org" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2b09d376-ae95-4682-85cd-76a3049c7a6c" /><br>
<br>
2. On the Downloads page, click the link labeled "Windows Hosts". (If you are using a device other than the school provided laptop, choose the option that matched your Operating System and Hardware configuration.)<br>
<br>
<img width="1920" height="919" alt="Download_Virtualbox" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8e012cb0-0ad3-4f66-a2e2-230354199119" /><br>
<br>
3. Once the installer has finished downloading, go ahead and click on it to launch it.(Where you look may vary based on your web browser. I use FireFox and the downloads show up in the upper right corner.)<br>
<br>
<img width="463" height="123" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fe20ac4a-9811-4fff-b96e-9c98d821817b" /><br>
<br>
4. If you encouter the User Account Control (UAC) pop up, go ahead and click "Yes".<br>
<br>
<img width="479" height="414" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/48600e0e-ed4a-4576-890f-0fcf7f440688" /><br>
<br>
5. You may get an error message stating that VirtualBox needs the Microsoft Visual C++ 2019 redistributeable. If so, click <a href="https://github.com/anthonyqueenmycc/SoYouWannaHomelab/tree/main/01-Installing_VirtualBox/01.01-Installing%20C%2B%2B" target="_blank"> Here </a> and follow the instructions. If you do not receive this error, continue on to step 6. <br>
<br>
<img width="371" height="241" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8eba8bf9-f6da-405a-aa4e-c252e28793fa" /><br>
<br>
<section id="resume_install">
6. You will be greeted by the VirtualBox Installation Wizard. Go ahead and click "Next".<br>
<br>
<img width="503" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/26694c30-d883-490f-be0b-8b5d328d24f2" /><br>
<br>
7. On the End-User License Agreement (EULA) page, click to accept the terms, and then click "Next".<br>
<br>
<img width="506" height="402" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dd09e85d-89f2-4fc8-bf75-3d630771dc22" /><br>
<br>
8. On the Custom Setup page, leave all the settings as they are, and click "Next".<br>
<br>
<img width="503" height="399" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a1dd826-5223-4f84-ae8a-106956902ef6" /><br>
<br>
9. You will receive a warning about a Network Disconnection. Go ahead and click "Yes"<br>
<br>
<img width="501" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c0df17cf-8bc3-427d-8fc5-bc8bfb992e96" /><br>
<br>
10. There may be a message about missing dependencies, but you can ignore this for now, and just click "Yes" to continue the installation.<br>
<br>
<img width="500" height="398" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9dad3034-0c25-4063-ba34-667ffe77eaff" /><br>
<br>
11. On the Custom Setup page, leave everything as it is, and click "Next".<br>
<br>
<img width="501" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e197f622-d709-4951-988e-56f18de65578" /><br>
<br>
12. On the Ready to Install page, click "Install"!<br>
<br>
<img width="501" height="399" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c1df5cb0-1783-44f0-85e5-81205a839a99" /><br>
<br>
13. Wait just a few minutes while the software is installed. You may lose your internet connection for just a moment, this is normal.<br>
<br>
14. Once the installation is complete, click "Finish".<br>
<br>
<img width="501" height="399" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52d03665-21ba-4a89-a7ef-78f6d1ee6d7b" /><br>
<br>
15. Congratulations! You have successfully taken your first step into creating a Homelab!<br>
